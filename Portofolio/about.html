Visiting Fozendha Senga

Discover the Joy of Horseback Riding! Looking for an unforgettable experience? Visit Fozendha Senga and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature while bonding with majestic horses. Whether you're an experienced rider or a beginner, we have something for everyone!

Supporting Students with School Materials

I’m excited to share that my project aimed at supporting students by providing them with essential school materials has been successfully completed! Thanks to the incredible support from JMF members, we were able to distribute 400 sets of school supplies to students in need. This initiative has empowered many young learners, ensuring they have the tools they need to succeed in school. I’m grateful to everyone who contributed and made this project possible!
